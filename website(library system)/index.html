    Introduction


    The Computerized Library system with Qr Code is a software system is designed to manages, and easily processes all the students who want to borrow books from the library. With an auto-generated Qr Code, it will be easier to identify the student who has already borrowed the book and managed all the books. Using a Scanner, it will be easy to update the records of students who want to return the book they already borrowed.

    This software system project, developed by VB10 and MySQL Database, was mainly developed for students and librarian of the College of Science and Technology Institute to manage the library books, students records, and any related features. It can help the school librarian to track the students who borrow or return books and generate some reports. The system software has a pleasant User Interface and User-Friendly features and functionalities.

    BackGround


    On September 20, 2023, researchers implemented the library system at the College of Science and Technology Institute using a QR code to identify the specific student who borrowed the book. Also to manage and analyze all the books and records. This can be attributed to the ease of finding information with one click rather than spending time searching books. The primary issue for any library system is organization and record-keeping.

    Due to the large number of book items, there's a chance of misplacing books due to the frequent movement of books within or outside of the library. The struggle for librarians and students to find the books. To overcome all the difficulties of librarians and users and improve the overall functioning of libraries, a QR code-based library management system is introduced.

    Benefits


    The Library System featuring QR Code is a software that is made for user convenience at the College of Science and Technology Institute Caloocan branch. Our user-friendly system is tailored to streamline record retrieval, significantly reducing time consumption for students during book borrowing and return transactions with librarians.

    Our user-friendly approach aims to significantly reduce time consumption for students, ensuring a seamless experience when navigating library transactions. Through the swift scanning of QR Codes, students can effortlessly locate and catalog their records, providing a convenient solution for those who regularly borrow books. Furthermore, the system goes beyond mere expediency, automatically saving transaction details post QR Code scan, eliminating the need for manual logging.

    This technology not only streamlines administrative processes but also fosters an environment conducive to academic pursuits. By minimizing administrative hassles, students can redirect their focus toward more meaningful engagement with academic resources.
