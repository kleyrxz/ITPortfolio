    Form Functionalities

    Log In Form

    This Log in form is the system security featured. Only the admin or Librarian can use it, to protect the records and information inside of the system.



    DashBoard Form

    In the dashboard, you can see the contents of the system, which include the library, book borrowed, book returned, and log-out.



    Library Form

    In the library form, you can see the lists of students' records for who borrowed books and what books they borrowed.



    Add Book Form

    In the Add Book form, it need to fill up the details of students who borrowed a book. It includes the book number, book title, author, publish date, and type of book to identify the specific book and person who borrowed the book.



    Borrow Form

    In the Borrow Form, the librarian will provide QR codes for students who borrowed books.



    Return Form

    Return Book process, once they return the book, the librarian will scan the book's QR code and the student information will automatically appear and be saved.
